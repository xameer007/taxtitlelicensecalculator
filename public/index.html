What is Tax Title and License (TTL)?

Tax Title and License (TTL) fees represent the total cost of taxes and government fees you must pay when purchasing and registering a vehicle. These costs are separate from the vehicle's purchase price and vary significantly by state.

What's Included in TTL Fees?

  • Sales Tax: State and local taxes on the vehicle purchase price (varies by state: 0%-10%+)
  • Title Fee: One-time fee to transfer legal ownership ($15-$100+ depending on state)
  • Registration Fee: Annual or biennial fee to register the vehicle ($20-$500+ based on state rules)
  • License Plates: Cost of new license plates if needed ($10-$50 typical)
  • Additional Fees: Processing fees, emissions fees, EV fees, county fees (varies by state)
